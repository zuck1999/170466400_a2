Cache

A Web cache is a technological temporary storage system that stores different types of Web documents, such as Web pages and other Web multimedia like images and videos. The purpose of these caches is to ultimately reduce server lag as it is quicker to get files from your hard drive than through the Internet, browser cache is essential another place on your hard drive where browsers keep things it has downloaded in case it is needed again. Clearing your cookies resets the MailChimp form because caches normally save all your cookies so certain forms and other registation add ons won't appear after the first use, so clearing the cookies allows the form to be active again cause your computer no longer has the saved information anymore.
