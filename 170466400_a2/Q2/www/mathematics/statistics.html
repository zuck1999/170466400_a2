Statistics

Course description- This course focuses on the practical methods of Statistics being: descriptive statistics; univariate models such as binomial, Poisson, uniform and normal.

Professors Bio - Florence Nightingale helped established much needed order and method within the hospital's statistical records. While collecting a lot of new data, Nightingale learned that poor sanitary practices were the main culprit of high mortality in hospitals. With her analysis, Nightingale revolutionized the idea that social phenomena could be objectively measured and subjected to mathematical analysis.
